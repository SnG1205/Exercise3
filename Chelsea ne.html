Good morning and welcome along to football.london's Chelsea news and transfer news live blog.

It could be a busy summer of transfer activity at Stamford Bridge.

Having guided the Blues to a sensational Champions League triumph, a tilt at the Premier League title will be firmly on the agenda for Thomas Tuchel next season.

He wants to put his own stamp on the squad he inherited from Frank Lampard and add fresh faces, especially up front, with goals having been a problem for the Blues this season.

Stick with us throughout the day on this live blog for all the very latest Chelsea transfer news and developments as they happen.

14:26George Smith

Chelsea want Villarreal defender

Chelsea are leading the race to sign Villarreal defender Alfonso Pedraza, a report by Napoli24 claims.

The left-back was a shining light for the Spanish club during the 2020/21 campaign and played a key role in their UEFA Europa League success.

The 25-year-old, who has played in England in the past with Leeds United, is also attracting interest from Napoli.

14:25Scott Trotter

Transfer analysis and more

12:59George Smith

Haaland prediction

Former Tottenham Hotspur midfielder Jermaine Jenas is confident that Chelsea will win the race to sign Borussia Dortmund star Erling Haaland.

The Blues are desperate to lay their hands on a striker this summer and Haaland is believed to be their top target.

Jenas said: "Chelsea are the only football club that could get Erling Haaland tomorrow.

"That is how their model works. The Haaland deal is very complicated. There are a lot of payments, the dad, the agent, then you have to get the top fee to get him out from Borussia Dortmund this year.

"If you wait a year you get him for significantly less than you would now. But you are in a competitive market where it will drag the price higher."

12:33George Smith

Inter want Alonso

Chelsea left-back Marcos Alonso is Simone Inzaghi's top target to bolster Inter Milan's left-sided defensive problems this summer.

Alonso has fallen down the pecking order at Stamford Bridge in the last year or so because of England star Ben Chilwell and is unlikely to feature much under Thomas Tuchel moving forward.

The latest reports claim Inter are keen to lure him to Serie A and is their top target to bolster the left-back berth.

11:37George Smith

Abraham approach knocked back

Turkish newspaper Turkish newspaper Sabah claims that Besiktas have seen a loan bid rejected for Chelsea striker Tammy Abraham.

The Blues forward faces the prospect of leaving Stamford Bridge this summer after falling down the pecking order under Thomas Tuchel.

Besiktas are keen to sign a striker this summer and have made a move for Abraham. However, the Blues are said to have turned down the Turkish giants' approach to land the England international on loan for the campaign.

10:25George Smith

Isak could be Chelsea's answer

After starring for Sweden at Euro 2020 last night, many Chelsea supporters are talking about the prospect of the club signing Alexander Isak this summer.

Although he didn't score against Spain, he made himself a real nuisance in attack and caused problems.

Could he be the ideal alternative to Erling Haaland?

09:12George Smith

Milan close in on Tomori

Chelsea defender Fikayo Tomori is close to joining AC Milan, according to reports on Italy.

Tomori spent the second half of this season on loan in Milan and impressed massively.

Now, he looks set to join the Serie A club on a permanent basis.

FULL STORY.

08:21George Smith

Hakimi decision

Inter Milan star Achraf Hakimi has reportedly decided that he wants his future to lie at Paris Saint-Germain, amid Chelsea's interest in his services.


Reports in France say that Hakimi has his heart set on linking-up with Mauricio Pochettino in Paris and will snub a move to Stamford Bridge.


FULL STORY.


07:50George Smith

Duo join race for youngster

Both AFC Wimbledon and Scottish outfit Motherwell have joined the race to sign Chelsea youngster Luke McCormick, according to reports.

The youngster is poised to leave Stamford Bridge on loan this summer once again after impressing whilst at Bristol Rovers last term.

Championship side Millwall are another club keen on the 22-year-old's signature.

Tashan Deniran-Alleyne

Hakimi bid increased

Chelsea have reportedly increased a bid for Inter wing-back Achraf Hakimi.

Apparently, the Blues are also considering including a player swap to sweeten the deal with the Italian club.

Inter are looking at a period of financial difficulty despite winning Serie A this season, with Antonio Conte already leaving the club this summer.

Haaland message

Chelsea might have been a little worried to have seen a video emerge on social media showing Erling Haaland partying with City’s Riyad Mahrez on Monday evening.

The video itself shows the two players dancing whilst on holiday on the Greek island of Mykonos.

Even Mahrez himself got in on the fun, quoting the original tweet and captioning it “Agent Mahrez on duty”.

However, he appeared to quash any talk of a potential move to Manchester City with a follow-up tweet. He said: “Just having fun calm down”.
